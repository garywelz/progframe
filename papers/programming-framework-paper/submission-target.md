## Wiley *Author Guidelines* — summary (as published)

### Open access and charges

- **Gold Open Access** from **1 January 2025**.
- Submissions received from **22 August 2024** onward: if accepted and published, an **APC** applies unless a **waiver** / discount applies (institution, funder, country waiver, etc.). See the journal’s **Open Access** page on Wiley for the current APC and licence terms.

### Submissions portal and support

- **Submit and track** via **Wiley Authors** — [submission.wiley.com](https://submission.wiley.com) → **My Submissions**.
- **Technical help:** [Wiley submission FAQs](https://authorservices.wiley.com/author-resources/Journal-Authors/submission-review/submit-your-paper.html) or **submissionhelp@wiley.com**.
- **Free Format submission:** format references and layout as you prefer for review; if accepted, Wiley will reformat to journal style. Stay **consistent**; include everything needed for ethical peer review (ORCIDs, co-author details, statements).

### Manuscript file (initial review)

- Full article as **Word** (or similar) with **figures and tables embedded** for review.
- At **revision**, figures are submitted as **separate image files** per Wiley figure guidance (**review** vs **post-acceptance** requirements differ — follow linked FAQs from the journal page).
- **LaTeX:** Wiley **NJD** template recommended; upload `.tex` as “Main Document – LaTeX .tex File” and a **PDF** as “Main Document - LaTeX PDF”; supporting files as “LaTeX Supplementary File”.
- **Revised submissions:** supply a **track-changes** main document.
- **Optional:** author headshots (community / identity on site).

### Article types and limits

| Type | Word limit | Notes |
|------|------------|--------|
| **Original article** | **3,000–6,000** | Hypothesis/questions, methods, findings, discussion of inference; builds on prior work; **≥2 external reviewers**; **suggest reviewers** (editors may choose others). |
| **Industry update** | 2,000–4,000 | Not promotional; contextualise alternatives. |
| **Case study** | 2,000–4,000 | Implementation / lessons; not promotional. |
| **Opinion** | 1,000–4,000 | Scholarly; referenced as appropriate. |

### Original article — required ingredients

1. **Title page:** authors, affiliations, **emails**, **ORCID** (required by this journal), ethics/integrity statements as applicable; photographs optional.
2. **Abstract:** **fewer than 200 words** — findings-focussed (“we did this, we found that”), **not** an introduction.
3. **Key point/s:** **2–6** bullets, **one sentence each**, ~**140 characters** per point (used in the article and for promotion).
4. **Body:** structure as suitable (e.g. Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, Acknowledgements).
5. **Figures and tables** with captions; **references** per style below.
6. **No footnotes** — use references / notes in reference list per APA.
7. **Supplementary material** only if appropriate.
8. **Style:** short sentences, **first person**, **active voice**, headings to break up text; audience is **publishing professionals** as well as academics.

### Style, spelling, references

- **Spelling:** **Oxford UK** (e.g. *colour*, *analyse*, *organisation*, *centre*; *program* for computing). See [University of Oxford Style Guide (PDF)](https://www.ox.ac.uk/sites/files/oxford/media_wysiwyg/University%20of%20Oxford%20Style%20Guide.pdf).
- **References:** **APA** (in-text: *Leiter & Maslach, 1998*; three+ authors: *Smith et al., 2020*). **Full journal titles**, **DOIs** where possible. Guide: [Purdue OWL APA](https://owl.purdue.edu/owl/research_and_citation/apa_style/apa_formatting_and_style_guide/general_format.html).
- **General websites** named in prose need not go in the reference list if cited inline (example given on site: ALPSP URL in text only).

### Ethics, review, data, preprints

- **Peer review:** **single-anonymized** standard; reviewers anonymous to authors unless they opt to sign.
- **Suggest reviewers** where the article type allows; editors decide.
- **Preprints:** allowed — **declare** at submission; update preprint with link to final article when published.
- **Data:** expect to share underlying data (repository + citation, or supplementary material; supp PDFs are limited for readers). **Data availability statement** — use Wiley’s options.
- **Conflict of interest**, **funding**, **permissions** for third-party material, **IRB / consent** as applicable — follow the journal page and linked Wiley ethics / reporting guidelines.
- **Plagiarism check:** iThenticate CrossCheck.
- **Authorship:** ICMJE criteria; **CRediT** contributorship encouraged.
- **ORCID:** **required** for this journal.

### Artificial intelligence / LLM use

If generative AI tools contributed to any portion of the manuscript, describe use **transparently** in **Methods** and/or **Acknowledgements**. Authors remain responsible for accuracy and citation. Tools used only for spelling, grammar, light editing are out of scope of this rule. AI tools cannot be listed as authors (COPE-aligned policy on page).

- **Licence:** open access with **Creative Commons** (exact CC variant on journal OA page; check funder mandates).
- **Proofs:** HTML proofs; **48-hour** return typical; check renumbered tables/figures/refs and legend vs text.
- **Continuous publication:** articles publish online with final citation when ready (not held for issue pagination).
- **Editorial / production contacts (examples from page):** **learnedpublishing@wiley.com** (e.g. name change); **LEAP@wiley.com** (production / authorship corrections per their instructions).
