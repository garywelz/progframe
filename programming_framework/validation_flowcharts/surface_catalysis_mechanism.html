The process shows gas phase reactants and reaction conditions as inputs, catalyst surface preparation and characterization methods, surface reaction operations including adsorption, surface diffusion, and surface reactions, intermediate surface species and product formation, and final catalytic performance assessment. This flowchart serves as the foundation for Experiment 3 validation, where framework predictions of surface reaction mechanisms will be compared against experimental surface spectroscopy and kinetic data.

Validation Metrics

This flowchart supports the following validation metrics for Experiment 3:

  • Rate-Determining Step Identification: Framework correctly identifies adsorption, surface reaction, or desorption as rate-limiting
  • Adsorption Energy Prediction: Predicted adsorption energies within 10% of experimental values
  • Surface Intermediate Prediction: Successful prediction of surface intermediate species and their stability
  • Catalytic Activity Optimization: Framework optimization leads to improved catalytic activity and selectivity

Experimental Application

This flowchart guides the experimental validation by:

  1. Identifying key surface chemistry parameters (temperature, pressure, surface composition)
  2. Predicting surface reaction mechanisms based on framework analysis
  3. Providing a systematic approach to surface spectroscopy analysis
  4. Establishing clear success criteria for validation

Surface Chemistry Details

The flowchart captures the key steps of surface catalysis:

  • Surface Preparation: Catalyst cleaning, reconstruction, and characterization
  • Reactant Adsorption: Gas phase molecules adsorbing to active surface sites
  • Surface Diffusion: Adsorbed species moving across the catalyst surface
  • Surface Reaction: Chemical transformation of adsorbed species
  • Product Desorption: Reaction products leaving the surface

Surface Analysis Techniques

The framework integrates with key surface analysis methods:

  • XPS (X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy): Surface composition analysis
  • TPD (Temperature Programmed Desorption): Adsorption energy measurement
  • STM (Scanning Tunneling Microscopy): Surface structure imaging
  • IR Spectroscopy: Surface intermediate identification
